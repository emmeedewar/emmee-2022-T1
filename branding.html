BRANDING

cleanfrog

Founded by a South Australian creative in late 2019,cleanfrog was created from experimenting with scrap fabrics and a secondhand sewing machine on the kitchen table. The spike of popularity of the hoppy amphibians meant that cleanfrog expanded quite rapidly from one-off Depop commissions to a full Etsy store! Perfect for gifts for your froggy loving significant other or delicatley scented hand warmers for the winter, these little pals are custom made to be a lifelong friend.
